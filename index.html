Cultivating Competency in STEM

OBJECTIVE

Overarching goal of the Königsberg Bridges Institute

UNLEASH POSSIBILITY

To bring out the latent potential and power of possibility through programs and infrastructure for education in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics ("STEM").

VISION

Long-term direction of the Königsberg Bridges Institute, i.e. guiding principles

MORALITY

To address evolving ethical issues in STEM by promoting cooperation between academia, industry, government, and the people.

QUALITY

To help individuals, groups, and organizations build trust and appreciation in sciences and technologies for discouraging populist anti-intellectualism through the empathetic presentation and discernment of STEM concepts.

COLLECTIVITY

To collaborate with academia, industry, and government to develop and support shared utilities and infrastructure for international and intergenerational initiatives.

ACCESSIBILITY

To provide accessible educational resources and programs to individuals, groups, and organizations of any learning level who are interested in STEM with first principles thinking.

MISSION

Medium-term direction of the Königsberg Bridges Institute, i.e. for 2020s decade

EMPOWERING OUR YOUTH

To help young people find purpose by introducing STEM in breadth and depth so that they may make informed decisions on their careers.

ENCOURAGE EXPLORATION

To support academic exchanges and international student relationships for school and work.

ELEVATE ENTREPRENEURS

To seek out promising STEM entrepreneurs and provide them with access to resources for prototyping and scaling solutions.

OUR STEM COMPETITION FOUNDATIONS

For practical solution design addressing real-world challenges using web3: distributed ledgers and decentralized applications

For discovering data science insights from data analysis to novel applications of current and emerging machine learning capabilities
